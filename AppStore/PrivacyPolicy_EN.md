# Privacy Policy - CourrierCode

**Last updated: January 15, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to CourrierCode. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

## Summary

**CourrierCode does NOT collect, store, or share ANY personal data.**

## Data Collection

### Data we DO NOT collect:

- ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contacts or address book
- ❌ Photos or files
- ❌ Encoded or decoded messages
- ❌ Secret codes used
- ❌ Usage history
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Browsing data
- ❌ Device information

### Local Operation

All CourrierCode operations are performed **locally on your device**:

- Message encoding and decoding happen entirely on your iPhone/iPad
- Secret codes and random tables never leave your device
- No Internet connection is required to use the application
- No data is sent to our servers or third parties

### Local Storage

The only data stored locally on your device (via UserDefaults) are:

- Your secret code to unlock the reference table
- The random table code if you have activated one

This data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

CourrierCode does not integrate any third-party services:

- ❌ No advertising
- ❌ No analytics
- ❌ No tracking SDKs
- ❌ No integrated social networks
- ❌ No cloud services

## Security

Although we do not collect any data, we take security seriously:

- The application works entirely offline
- No data transmission over the Internet
- Source code developed according to iOS best practices

## Children

CourrierCode is suitable for all ages (4+) and does not collect any information about children or adults.
